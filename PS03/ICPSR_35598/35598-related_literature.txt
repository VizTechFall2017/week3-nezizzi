Bibliography
The aDvANCE Project: A Study of Career Transition for Professional Dancers
[2003]

This bibliography includes published and unpublished works that are related 
to ICPSR study 35598. It includes works that are based on primary
or secondary analysis of the data, or which describe or critique those
data or the collection methodology. 

If you publish a work or know of other works that are based on these data,
please send the complete citation and name of the ICPSR study used to:
icpsr-biblio-notify@umich.edu 


1. Baumol, William J.; Jeffri, Joan; Throsby, David, "Making Changes:
Facilitation the Transition of Dancers to Post-Performance Careers." The
aDvANCE Project Research Report. New York, NY: Columbia University,
Research Center for Arts and Culture, 2004.
Full text PDF: http://www.cpanda.org/data/a00191/changes.pdf

2. Jeffri, Joan, " Life After Dance: Career Transition of Professional
Dancers. International journal of arts management." International Journal
of Arts Management. 2006, 8, (3), 54.
Full text PDF: http://www.cpanda.org/data/a00191/changes.pdf

3. Jeffri, Joan, "After the ball is over." International Journal of
Cultural Policy. Nov 2005, 11, (3), 341 - 355.
Full text PDF: http://www.cpanda.org/data/a00191/changes.pdf

4. Levine, Mindy N., "Beyond Performance: Building a Better Future for
Dancers and the Art of Dance." International Organization for the
Transition of Professional Dancers, 2004.
Full text PDF: http://www.iotpd.org/upl/advocacy-report-beyond-performance-pdf.pdf

5. Simmons, Laura Kei, "After the Curtain Call: The Career Transition

Full text PDF: http://www.iotpd.org/upl/advocacy-report-beyond-performance-pdf.pdf
